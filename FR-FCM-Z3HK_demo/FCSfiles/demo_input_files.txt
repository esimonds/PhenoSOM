Q. What goes in this subfolder?
A. Visit FlowRepository accession ID FR-FCM-Z3HK and download the FCS files from the experiment with "Tcellpanel" in the filename. There should be 39 FCS files totaling about 2.86GB. Move these to the FCSfiles subfolder.

Q. How can I be sure I have the right files?
A. Refer to "Demo input verification/FR-FCM-Z3HK_demo_FCS_input_MD5_hashes.md5"  You can should obtain md5 checksums for the files on your local hard drive and make sure they match this file.
